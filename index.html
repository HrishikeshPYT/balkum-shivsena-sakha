About Us

Shiv Sena (Uddhav Balasaheb Thackeray) [UBT] is a Hindutva-based, Marathi regionalist, nationalist political party formed in 2022 under the leadership of former Chief Minister of Maharashtra, Uddhav Thackeray. Our focus is on social conservatism, Marathi pride, and Hindutva values, with a goal of empowering the people of Maharashtra.

Our Initiatives

We are actively involved in community welfare programs aimed at improving lives. Our key initiatives include:

  • Healthcare Programs: Offering free medical camps and check-ups for underprivileged communities.
  • Education Support: Providing scholarships and educational resources to students.
  • Women Empowerment: Conducting workshops and vocational training for women to promote equality.
  • Environmental Conservation: Organizing tree plantation drives and cleanliness campaigns to protect the environment.
  • Community Development: Building essential infrastructure and offering services to improve rural and urban communities.

Leadership

Our leadership is dedicated to advancing our cause and bringing positive change to society. Meet our leaders:
